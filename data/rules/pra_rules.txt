CRR Article 26 – Capital instruments
Paid-up ordinary shares shall be classified as Common Equity Tier 1 capital instruments
where they are issued directly by the institution and fully paid up.

CRR Article 26(1)(c) – Retained earnings
Retained earnings shall be included in Common Equity Tier 1 capital.
This includes interim or year-end profits that have not been distributed.

CRR Article 36 – Deductions from CET1
Institutions shall deduct intangible assets, including goodwill,
from Common Equity Tier 1 capital.

CRR Article 50 – Calculation of CET1
Common Equity Tier 1 capital equals the sum of capital instruments
and retained earnings, minus regulatory deductions.
